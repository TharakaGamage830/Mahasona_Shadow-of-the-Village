# 🕯️ The Storyteller’s Script: A Journey through Yaksha Village

*This guide is written for the **Vedamahattaya** (Storyteller) to narrate the game, or for players to understand the flow as an unfolding horror story.*

---

## 🌅 Prologue: The Gathering
"Welcome, souls. You have come from far and wide to this cursed village. The air is thick with the scent of burning incense and damp earth. Look at the faces around you—friends, neighbors, kin. But know this: one of you is no longer human. One of you carries the hunger of the **Mahasona**."

**What happens:** Everyone logs in and gathers in the Lobby.

---

## 🏮 The Secret Seal (Role Reveal)
"As I pass the ritual cards, look at your reflection in the dark water. Do not let your eyes betray you. Whether you are a protector of the light or a servant of the dark, your secret is your life. Keep it well."

**What happens:** Players reveal their Role Cards. The tension begins as everyone starts to wonder who is lying.

---

## 🌑 Part I: The Descent (The Night Phase)
"The sun dips below the horizon. The cicadas go silent. A heavy mist rolls in from the jungle. Close your eyes, villagers... sleep, if you can. For the spirits are waking."

### 1. The Yaka’s Mischief (Selected Yaka)
"The Yaka steps from the shadows. If it is Kalu Kumaraya, he bends the paths between houses, swapping the souls within. If it is Riri Yaka, he breathes a sickly red mist, poisoning a mind and corrupting their magic."

### 2. The Seer’s Vision (Kattandiya)
"The Kattandiya kneels before the spirit flame. He asks the ancestors: *'Is this soul pure or tainted?'* The flame flickers... showing him the red skull of evil or the white lamp of life."

### 3. The Holy Shield (Pirith Monk)
"The Pirith Monk chants his ancient verses. He draws a circle of holy ash around one door. No demon can cross this line tonight. The soul inside is safe."

### 4. The Final Hunt (Mahasona)
"Finally... the Mahasona rises. He is ten feet of fur and bone, his eyes glowing like embers. He stalks the paths, choosing his prize. A single claw scratches a door. A life is claimed."

---

## ☀️ Part II: The Daybreak (The Town Square)
"Open your eyes. The sun is cold and pale. You gather in the square, counting the survivors... but one heart has stopped beating. [Player Name] has been taken. The ritual has failed them."

**What happens:** The village sees who died. If the **Vedda Hunter** was killed, he tells one last story (takes a shot).

---

## ⚖️ Part III: The Gam Maduwa (The Tribunal)
"The whispers begin. *'I saw him moving in the dark!' 'Her eyes look different!'* You must decide. If you do not execute the demon, he will eat you one by one. But take care... if you burn an innocent, you do the demon's work for him."

**What happens:** Players talk and nominate.
"Someone has been accused! [Nominee Name], stand before the fire. Villagers, cast your votes. Will you **Spare** this soul, or will you **Burn** the evil out?"

---

## 🏁 The Conclusion: Win or Perish

### 🌿 If the Mahasona is Burned:
"The fire roars! As the flames consume the accused, a terrible screech echoes through the valley. The Mahasona's shadow flees back into the jungle. The curse is broken. The village is saved."

### 🩸 If the Village is Overrun:
"The sun sets again, and there are too few of you left to fight. The Mahasona drops his disguise and stands tall in the center of the square. One by one, the lanterns go out. The village belongs to the Yakshas now."

---
*Will you survive the night, or will your name be forgotten in the mist?* 🏮⛓️
